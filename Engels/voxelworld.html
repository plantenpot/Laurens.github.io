What is Voxel World?

Voxel World is a small building game I made on a website called Roblox. Roblox is a website with thousands of games all made by users of the website. In Voxel World you are able to build anything you can imagine with small 1x1 blocks. I made this game purely for education and fun. The whole game has been programmed in Lua.
