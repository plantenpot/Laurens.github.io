Malaysia Covid Vaccination Map

What is this Malaysia Covid vaccinations tracker about?

This is a small project I had to make in school together with another classmate. My classmate mainly focused on the front-end while I did the back-end. On this map you can see all the different states of Malaysia, you can hover over the states with your mouse to find out about their names. When you click on a state you will see all the data about the population and the total the amount of vaccinated people divided in a 18-59 and 60-above group.

The goal of this project was learning how to work with webservices.
